My Life

A little about my life! 9/14/2025

I was born and raised in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. I’m a big foodie, I love to travel, and I enjoy spending time in nature, especially rock climbing. When I’m not out exploring, I like to relax with my cat, Venus. The photos you’ll see are snapshots of my life, from meals I’ve tried to places I’ve visited, and of course plenty of pictures of Venus.

Places I want to visit! 9/14/2025

I would love to take a worldwide tour of Asia. Japan is at the top of my list, especially for the food and even the convenience stores. I also dream of traveling to places full of nature and rocks to climb, while exploring and learning from many different cultures along the way.

  • Author: Evie Le

My values and inspirations 9/14/2025

I aspire to become the best version of myself and to learn everything I can in life. I want to create a good life for both me and my cat, Venus, and live in a way that leaves no room for regrets.
